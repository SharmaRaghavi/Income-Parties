How Much Money Did Indian National Political Parties Get in 2020-21?

By Raghavi Sharma

According to a report by the Association for Democratic Reforms , the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) declared a total income of 752.337 crore Indian rupees to the Election Commission of India during Financial Year 2020-21 and spent 82% of the total income.

The BJP’s total income thus accounts more than half or about 54% of the total income of the eight National Parties during FY 2020-21.

However, even so, the BJP’s annual income had actually decreased by 79% from a whopping Rs 3623.28 crore during FY 2019-20 to Rs 752.337 crore during FY 2020-21.
